Help & FAQ

Last updated: April 15, 2026

How do I create an account?

You can register using email/password or sign in with Google. Complete the onboarding steps to set your gym and schedule for better matches.

How do I update my profile information?

Open the app, go to Settings > Profile, and edit the fields you want. Changes are saved to your account immediately.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

Use the in-app report feature on a user's profile or a game detail page. Reports are reviewed and may result in account action.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps. This permanently removes your data.

Where can I see safety guidance?

See our Community Safety & Public Availability Guidelines for tips on meeting new users safely.
